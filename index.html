Projects

Empathy and redemption: Exploring the narrative transformation of online support for mental health across communities before and after Covid-19
Yuxuan Cai, Ertong Wei, Xintong Cai
PLOS ONE, 2024
In this project, we examined the evolving dynamics of mental health support within Reddit communities throughout the COVID-19 pandemic. Leveraging large language models (LLMs), we annotated and analyzed the changing narratives in these virtual communities, revealing significant shifts in empathy and support. Our dataset contains a total of 5,216,505 original submission posts, providing a robust foundation that enhances the credibility of our research.
